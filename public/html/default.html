Welcome to ModEvo

ModEvo is a novel mathematical modeling software for predicting trait evolution. We have developed three models of increasing complexity for predicting phenotype evolution. Model One: Classic uses a classic variance-covariance matrix (g-matrix). Model Two: Tanning was created to account for "developmental entanglement". Model Three: Tolerance extends consideration for non-additive interactions even further. Once you have selected a model, your inout will displayed here. You can switch between any of the three models, or any of your previous result sets by using the navigation bar on the left of the screen.
