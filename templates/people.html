Sean Beseler

Sean has been working with Filip since fall 2015. He's developing the normal and bivariate Gaussian distribution models which are used to generate sample populations in the lab's on-going co-variance work.

Tim Wood

Tim has worked with Filip (in on-going work with collaborator Brian Chen from Lehigh University) since the start of 2016. He's developed several python programs for processing and analyzing rigidity and cavity data for more than 80,000 of the X-ray crystallography resolves structures in the Protein Data Bank.

Ramesh Kumar

Ramesh is a graduate student working with Filip (in on-going work with collaborator Nurit Haspel from the University of Massachusetts) on a project to systematically determine which machine learning method(s) are best suited to identify from rigidity and biology data which residues in a biomolecule contribute most to the protein's stability.
